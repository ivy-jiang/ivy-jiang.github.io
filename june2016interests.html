What's interesting

Many interests and things to learn

Posted by Ivy Jiang on June 12, 2016

The summer time is a great time to sit down and relax, especially for us folks who are in school and still get a summer break as a great boon.

It’s a time when perhaps more thought could be put into interests outside of school, including hobbies and other passions. Apart from the time I spend working, I have been spending much of my summer developing such interests. For example, a friend and I have been brainstorming a food and beverage related enterprise. It’s fun to talk to a great friend about our shared interests, knowing that there is a possibility for it to materialize. We just have to work hard towards this goal of ours. There is an element of luck, like everything in life, but like everything in life, we need to do our best. There’s no denying we’ve got so much to learn. But we’re excited, and we’ve got each other. Hey, it's true!
