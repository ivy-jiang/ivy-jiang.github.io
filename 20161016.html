Preseason Rumble

Posted by Ivy Jiang on October 31, 2016

Not exactly pre-season predictions since the season has begun, but here are some fun guesstimates for the 2016-2017 NBA season:

  • - Spurs will have a better regular record the than the warriors. Spurs crushed the season opening matchup so it would not be unexpected news if this were to happen, though the warriors are still the favorites of many.
  • - MVP will go to LeBron or Westbrook. Probably LeBron. Difficult to give it to Steph or Kevin unless if one of them really carries the team but then, the entire point of Durant joining the team..
  • - Dwight regains some popularity playing for his hometown and makes the all-star team if he records double-doubles, especially in the generally less-competitive East
  • - Warriors/Cavs or Spurs/Cavs Final, though that is not saying much
  • - Harden brings the Rockets to the same kind of result in previous years- playoffs with some clutch movements but not really progressing further than the WCF
  • - Not sure what will happen to the Knicks but maybe not more than a first-round exit in the playoffs unless if Noah plays a substantial part. Will be a great improvement from last year regardless
  • - The Bulls will do better than the Knicks but maybe not that much better
  • - Probably the same fate for Boston as it had achieved last year and the year before that
  • - PG has a great year for the Pacers, but the Pacers won’t make it past the 2nd round probably
  • - JLin could make the all-star team if he continues to start and wins games for the Nets
  • - Maybe after this season, and sometime in the future, Westbrook and PG join forces in LA
  • - I don’t really know how good the Clippers are; it’s been CP3, Blake and DeAndre for some time now
  • - Haven’t really followed this season’s rookies and the sophomores, but too many injures among the young guys even before they step foot in an NBA game
