Starting the website

Posted by Ivy Jiang on June 12, 2016

A fair question is why am I making a website?

There are a few reasons. First of all, I am interested in the process of creating and maintaining the website itself. My interest in HTML/CSS started from my Neopets days when I enjoyed creating petpages for my Neopets. With my own page now, I am able to continue this inclination through updating the code and content. The HTML/CSS and bootstrap framework of this website is from Start Bootstrap with edits made by myself.

I also enjoy writing, and now that I have a place to congregate my thoughts, I am encouraged to write as a means of expression. As such, there are many topics of interest that I would like to be able to explore.

Primarily, I created this website because I find it to be a fun thing to do for leisure. All of the content may not necessarily be classified as leisure, but the process of having a virtual space is quite fun. I have lots to learn and I am excited. This is a space where I am able to channel and share some of that.
